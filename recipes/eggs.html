Grandma Bishop Eggs

2 Perfectly cooked eggs

Grandma Bishop eggs are better than yours. Period. I dare you or your loved ones to fry up an egg like this.

Ingredients

  • 2 farm fresh eggs
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. On medium heat, melt butter in cast iron skillet.
  2. When butter is melted and slightly bubbling, crack 2 eggs in pan. Salt and pepper should now be added, so it cooks in.
  3. When it feels right, flip eggs over. (You will know when this is. At least Grandma Bishop always did.)
  4. Remove eggs from pan and serve with toast and bacon.
  5. Enjoy the best eggs of your life
